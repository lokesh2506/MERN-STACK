Cheif Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For teh dougth:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

Instruction

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast,sugau,eggs
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cower with plastic wrap
